# MENTION

## Scope

These rules shall apply to this contract.

## Underlying

The Underlying for this Contract is words used in `<address>` by `<person>`.

Revisions to the Underlying made after Expiration will not be accounted for in determining the Expiration Value.

## Source Agency

The Source Agency is `<person>`, The New York Times, the Associated Press, Bloomberg News, Reuters, Axios, Politico, Semafor, The Information, The Washington Post, The Wall Street Journal, ABC, CBS, CNN, Fox News, MSNBC, and NBC.

## Type

The type of Contract is an **Event Contract**.

## Issuance

The Contract will be issued on an as-needed basis for Members.

---

## Definitions

### `<word>`

`<word>` refers to the particular word or phrase potentially to be mentioned by `<person>`.

It may also refer to:

* A word being said a minimum number of times
* One of multiple acceptable words or phrases (e.g., `"Doge/Dogecoin"`)
* A strike for `"Event does not occur before <date>"`

### `<address>`

`<address>` refers to a given statement, speech, briefing, address, or other form of speech given by `<person>`. It can be given in written or oral form.

### `<person>`

`<person>` refers to a given individual or individuals involved in politics (including, e.g., domestic and foreign legislators, heads of government and state, and prominent business executives).

---

## Payout Criterion

The Payout Criterion for the Contract encompasses the Expiration Values that `<word>` is stated by `<person>` during `<address>`, with the following stipulations:

### Included

* **Plural and possessive forms**

  * Example: If `<word>` is `Immigrant`, then `Immigrants` will count.
  * Example: If `<word>` is `Law and order`, then `Laws and order` will count.
  * Example: If `<word>` is `Eggs`, then `The eggs' shells are fragile` will count.

* **Compound words, including hyphenated forms**

  * Example: If `<word>` is `Palestine`, then `pro-Palestine` will count.
  * Example: If `<word>` is `couch`, then `couch potato` will count.

* **Ordinal forms (when `<word>` includes a number)**

  * Example: If `<word>` is `January 6`, then `January 6th` will count.

* **Homonyms**

  * Example: If `<word>` is `ICE`, then `ice water` will count.

* **Homographs**

  * Example: If `<word>` is `bass`, then `bass guitar` and `bass fishing` will count.

* **Words uttered in adjacent context**

  * Example: If `<word>` is `Elon / Musk`, then `Elon University` will count.

* **Non-standard transliterations to English (within reason)**

  * Example: If `<word>` is `Zelensky`, then any of `Zelenski`, `Zelenskii`, `Zelenskyy`, or `Zelenkiy` will count.

### Excluded

* **Grammatical and tense inflections**

  * Example: If `<word>` is `Immigrant`, then `Immigration` will *not* count.

* **Closed compound words or words that merely encompass the target**

  * Example: If `<word>` is `fire`, then `firetruck` will *not* count, but `fire station` will count.

* **Words uttered in another language**

  * Example: If `<word>` is `fire`, then `"This queso is fuego"` will *not* count.

* **Homophones**

  * Example: If `<word>` is `write`, then `write a letter` will count, but `right answer` will *not* count.

* **Similar words and synonyms**

  * Similar words and synonyms are *not* encompassed by the Payout Criterion.

## Event Non-Occurrence

If the event does not occur before `<date>`, then the strike for `"Event does not occur before <date>"` will resolve to **Yes**, and all other strikes will resolve to **No**.

---

## Contract Mechanics

* **Minimum Tick:** $0.01
* **Position Accountability Level:** $25,000 per strike, per Member
* **Last Trading Date:** Same as the Expiration Date
* **Last Trading Time:** Same as the Expiration Time
* **Settlement Date:** No later than the day after the Expiration Date, unless under review pursuant to Rule 7.1

---

## Expiration

* **Expiration Date:** The latest Expiration Date shall be the day after `<address>` occurs; if it does not occur, the Contract will expire on `<date>`. If an event described in the Payout Criterion occurs, or consensus is reached earlier, expiration may be moved earlier pursuant to Rule 7.2.

* **Expiration Time:** 10:00 AM ET

---

## Settlement

* **Settlement Value:** $1.00
* **Expiration Value:** The value of the Underlying as documented by the Source Agency on the Expiration Date at the Expiration Time.

---

## Contingencies

Before Settlement, Kalshi may, at its sole discretion, initiate the Market Outcome Review Process pursuant to Rule 6.3(d) of the Rulebook. If an Expiration Value cannot be determined on the Expiration Date, Kalshi has the right to determine payouts pursuant to Rule 6.3(b) of the Rulebook.
